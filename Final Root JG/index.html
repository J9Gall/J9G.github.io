Beach Battle

Football (soccer) is on the rise in Los Angeles with the city now hosting two powerhouses of the MLS. Now its time for you to show your city your skills with an added twist - on sand.

The Beach Battle will consist of three competition streams where teams will compete against one another in a knockout tournament. The winning teams will recieve a prize award of $5,000 and a selection of addidas training kits from the parley for the oceans range.

  • Mens 5 aside
  • Womens 5 aside
  • Mixed 14-18 year old 5 aside

All teams wishing to participate should complete the contact form. Once forms are recieved teams will recieve a confirmation with further details relating to registration times and required equipment.

Cre8 Los Angeles

We are looking for you to make this event your own by producing the music and kits for the beach battle.

Battle at the Beach aims to bring Los Angeles designers together to create a statement about their city. This year Adidas are calling for all creators to submit a project that they feel best represents their city - Los Angeles.

  • Audio submission (2mins min - 4mins max)
  • T-shirt design submission
  • Graphic Art (Wall Design)

You have no restrictions on what you can create but simply make it original (any submissions that are copyrighted will be removed and the author will be notified of the action taken)Shortlisted creations will be presented for the duration of the event with a winner from each category being selected

  • Winning audio submissions included in an Addidas advertisement
  • Winning T-shirt submissions will be made available for purchase in Addidas stores throughout LA
  • Winning graphic art will be transfered to wall in down town LA

Sustainable Los Angeles

Concerns for our environment are growing globaly and with that Adidas joined forces with Parley. This partnership has seen a growth in the use of recycled products from our oceans when creating new sport lines.

With the Sustainable City pLAn, the city of Los Angeles has committed itself to ddoing its bit to slow down environmental damange through reducing carbon emissions by 45% come 2025 and want to help in making that possible by making the beach battle a sustainable event. Therefore the soccer goals and balls will be made from recycled products sourced from the oceans. All food items will be served in biodegradable packaging.

To find out more about the various sustainable initiatives across the city please take a moment to visit the exhibition stalls located beside the beach soccer arena
